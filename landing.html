IT Frequently Asked Questions

When registering, enter only your network username (for example, first initial + last name), not your full email address. If it still doesn't register, please submit a Help Desk ticket.

Confirm you're connected to the 'Helio_Radius' Wi-Fi or the corporate network. Print jobs will not release from guest or external networks. Re-send the job after connecting and try again.

If it's regarding a loss of power, no connection to network/phones, adding another one, these are facilities requests.

Please do not open an IT ticket. Report suspicious messages using the Report Phishing button in Outlook. We routinely run phishing tests, and reporting via the Outlook tool is required.

Use the Company Portal app to install approved Helio apps. The Apple App Store is restricted on company devices; install from Company Portal instead.

Most users do not require VPN access. The VPN client is installed on all company devices, but access is granted only to specific roles (Operational Support and Leadership). If you believe you need access or are having trouble connecting, please submit a ticket. For step-by-step instructions, see the How-To on The Hub.
